ABOUT PEKAN KUNDASANG

Pekan Kundasang is a small town in Sabah, Malaysia, located at the foot of Mount Kinabalu. Famous for its cool weather and stunning views, it is a popular stop for visitors on their way to Mount Kinabalu National Park. The town is an important farming area where locals grow vegetables, fruits, and flowers, taking advantage of the fertile soil and cooler climate. It is also known for its natural attractions, such as hot springs and beautiful viewpoints, which draw tourists year-round. With agriculture and tourism as key parts of its economy, the town has grown in popularity while still maintaining its peaceful, rural charm.
