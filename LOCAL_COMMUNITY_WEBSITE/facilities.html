ACCOMMODATIONS

Crystal Hill R in Kundasang offers a peaceful, affordable stay with scenic views of Mount Kinabalu, perfect for a simple retreat, while Kinabalu Park (Mountain Lodge), located near the park's entrance, provides basic amenities in a nature-filled setting ideal for those wanting easy access to hiking trails. Mile 36 Lodge, a cozy, budget-friendly option, boasts great mountain views and friendly service, and Hotel Perkasa Kundasang, a mid-range hotel, offers comfort and convenience with a variety of rooms, making it perfect for travelers looking to explore the area and nearby attractions like Poring Hot Springs. All four accommodations provide a relaxing atmosphere in the cool highlands of Kundasang.
