KUNDASANG MARKET
In the center of Pekan Kundasang, this bustling market offers fresh produce, local handicrafts, and a chance to experience the culture and lifestyle of the local community.
KUNDASANG SPRING GARDEN
Situated nearby, this serene garden is filled with colorful flowers and offers a peaceful escape with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains, making it a perfect spot to relax and enjoy nature.
