PEKAN
KUNDASANG

Pekan Kundasang is a scenic town in Sabah, Malaysia, famous for its cool climate and stunning views of Mount Kinabalu. Known as the "Switzerland of Malaysia," it offers lush landscapes, local markets, and a peaceful getaway for nature lovers and adventurer.
