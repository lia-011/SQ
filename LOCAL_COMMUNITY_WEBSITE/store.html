CONVENIENCE STORE

88 Fresh Mart is a convenience store in Pekan Kundasang that typically operates with extended hours, often opening early in the morning and closing late at night, making it convenient for both early risers and those returning from activities in the area. In addition to its wide range of products, the store is known for its friendly staff, who can assist customers in finding what they need. The layout is generally organized, allowing for easy navigation through the aisles, whether you're looking for fresh produce, snacks, or household items.
