There are not a lot of healthcare or clinics available at Pekan Kundasang, Sabah. Only two clinics that are available and it is called Klinik Kundasang and Klinik Dr Faezah. While there a less to no information about Klinik Kundasang, Klinik Dr Faezah are availble to be contacted on Instagram. Klinik Dr. Faezah is a healthcare facility located in Pekan Kundasang, Sabah. The clinic operates from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. They offer services such as antenatal scans and general medical consultations.
